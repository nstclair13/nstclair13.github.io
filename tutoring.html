Option 1 - Individual Recorded Video Critique - $50 USD

Each recorded critique includes up to 30 minutes of review time on a demo reel, piece of animation, or a topic of your choosing. A private vimeo link will be provided allowing you to view the critique indefinitely.

Actual length of critique varies based on submission.

Option 2 - 15 Week Personalized Tutoring Program - $1,500 USD

We will work together to develop an idea, plan, and complete a polished animation from beginning to end (or more depending on skill level) during the 15 week tutorship. Each round of feedback will consist of a recorded and annotated critique for an assignment or animation test of your choosing.

Throughout the 15 week tutorship, I will be available to provide feedback on a regular, daily basais if so wished.

Any skill level is welcome, from beginner to advanced. Each 15 week tutorship is personalized and tailored to the artist's skill level.
