Frequently Asked Questions

Are you a school? Do you give grades or college credit?

No. These critiques are simply a source for receiving honest, yet critical feedback on your animation. My goal is to include a more affordable option that would provide supplemental support to other degree programs or online animation schools. No grades, no deadlines, no provided rigs or program requirements; just honest feedback and knowledge from an industry professional on your work.

How many assignments can I work on?

This depends on your skill level. A beginner may complete more smaller assignments where an intermediate or advanced student may complete one or two more complex projects. Each tutorship is custom tailored to each individual. I do suggest having two different shots to work on to maximize your time and money spent; this way when you are waiting on feedback for one project, you have something else to work on.

Are there any live lectures or meetings?

Unfortunately, No. All video feedback is pre-recorded and made available online for viewing via private Vimeo link. Daily written critiques and Google Chat is available for the 16 week 1 on 1 tutoring sessions.

When is feedback given? What is the structure of the tutorship?

Feedback is given whenever an assignment is submitted, generally within 24 hours. You are able to submit updates throughout the 16 weeks at your own pace. You can submit assignments every other day to once every week or once per month. While I am not able to provide daily video critiques due to my work schedule, I will provide written feedback if you do submit more regularly. One video critique per week will be uploaded provided you submit work for review.

Please be aware that I am loacted in Vancouver, BC Canada and on Pacific Time. Chances are there will be a time difference depending on where you are in the world (perhaps an entire 8+ hours). I often check out work in the evening after 8pm PST or during my lunch in the middle of the day.

What software is used?

Maya is recommended. I will critique any animation done in any software, however I generally only use Maya so I will not be able to provide technical specific details, animation file review, or helpful tips for any other programs.

A private Vimeo link will be provided to view each video critique.

What if I need time off? Do I lose time that I paid for?

Life happens, I understand that. Please contact me if something life changing happens or if you planned on taking a vacation during the 16 weeks. I will try to do my best to accommodate you and if needed, we can shift our schedule a week or two.
