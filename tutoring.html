Online Personalized Tutoring

Need a helpful critique with an animation test? Perhaps you are already in the industry & want to "level up" your skill set? Well, you've come to the right place!

I offer both individual pre-recorded video critiques & live one-on-one tutoring sessions covering a variety of topics including:

  • Demo Reel & Personal Assignment Critiques
  • Professional Workflow Tips & Tricks
  • Maya demonstrations and troubleshooting
  • Industry Advice
  • and much more!

Individual Recorded Video Critique - $35 USD

You submit an animation assignment of your choosing (Demo reels included!) & you will receive a pre-recorded video critique on your work with annotations and helpful advice.

* Actual length of critique varies based on submission, complexity & skill level.

Live 1 Hour Tutoring Session - $75 USD

Any skill level is welcome, from beginner to advanced. Each session is conducted live and is completely free-form. You choose the topic whether it's a critique, Maya demonstration, or just to chat about the industry. Each session is personalized to the artist's skill level and interest.



Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question? Browse below and hopefully it can be answered. If not, drop me a line below & I'll answer it!

How many assignments can I submit?

Please only submit one animation per individual pre-recorded critique. A sequence of shots that are tied together is no problem & if you would like a demo reel critique, I will gladly critique multiple assignments. That said, the critique will be much broader in scope. (ie. Which shots to include, which shots to remove, future assignment ideas, etc.)

When is feedback given? What are live session held?

Feedback is given generally within 24-48 hours for pre-recorded video critiques

If you sign up for a live session, we will establish a date and time for the session together.

Please be aware that I am loacted in Vancouver, BC Canada and on Pacific Standard Time. Chances are there will be a time difference depending on where you are in the world (perhaps an entire 8+ hours). I am generally available after 8pm PST & most weekends.

What software is used?

Maya is recommended. I will critique any animation done in any software, however I generally only use Maya so I will not be able to provide technical specific details, animation file review, or helpful tips for any other programs.

How much are the sessions?

Individual video critiques are $35 USD each.
Live One-on-One Tutoring sessions are $75 USD per hour.

How can I pay?

Payment is accepted through Google Wallet. Detailed payment information will be provided when signing up.

How do I sign up? When can I start?

Tutoring is ongoing & available year round. Please use the contact form below or email me to sign up!
