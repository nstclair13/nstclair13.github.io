    Hi there!

    My name is Nick. I'm an animator based out of Vancouver, B.C. currently working for Sony Pictures Imageworks.

    I've been working in the animation industry for nearly a decade in visual effects and feature animation. I have worked on a multitude of productions and have experience working in many styles of animation - from photorealistic to highly stylized. I am adept at keyframe work as well as performance capture editing. I also love sharing my expertise with others, if given the opportunity, as I have lead animation teams numerous times.

    I'm a graduate of AnimationMentor.com, iAnimate.net, and Southern New Hampshire University's Design Program. When I'm not spending my free time with my family and friends, you'll likely find me updating my blog, out riding my bike around the city, or knee deep in my garden at home.

    If you are interested in learning more specific details about the projects I've worked on, please take a moment to browse through my resume below. You can also find a printable version here.     

    I currently hold U.S. Citizenship as well as Canadian Permanent Residency.
